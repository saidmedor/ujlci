A few words about us.

We are 0 in this association.

UJLCI is a non-profit association of young Ivorian leaders, which works for the promotion of youth and civic values.

Upstream, UJLCI participates in political life in Côte d'Ivoire by sensitizing the youth to cultivate social cohesion, peace, union, discipline and work.

                  

The association acts in the fields of social, cultural and educational action.

                  

Its primary goal is personalized support for young people in order to guarantee their personal development, thus promoting their integration into society as an active and responsible citizen within it. She carries out her project through voluntary actions for the benefit of the community, training and individual project support, as part of social and / or professional integration processes.
